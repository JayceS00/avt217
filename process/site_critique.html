Navigation

The site's main navigation menu is clicking "ENTER SITE". By clicking this, it shows a clean labels: TOUR, SHOP, VIDEO, MUSIC, DIGITAL GARDEN, and SIGN-UP. These labels are concise guiding users directly to core areas.

Usability

The minimalist design delivers visually engaging experience for fans. However, absence of a search bar, and no visible "Home" link causes users might not easily backtrack. The clear, uncluttered layout reduces distractions.

Typography and hierarchy

The site uses strong headings and clean typography for minimalist aesthetic. There might be missed opportunities to emphasize hierachy within page content.

Overall style/look and feel

The site shows a clean, modern, and music centric vibe which matches with the band's aesthetic. The focused menu reflect a brand-forward, immersive approach. However, the "ENTER SITE" splash page and minimalist structure may be confusing who want instant content.
